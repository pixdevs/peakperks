Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I get started with planning my trip?

To begin planning your dream vacation, simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. One of our travel experts will contact you to understand your preferences, budget, and travel dates to create a personalized itinerary.

Do you charge a fee for your services?

We operate on a transparent fee structure based on the complexity and requirements of your trip. Our initial consultation is always complimentary, and we'll clearly outline any applicable fees before you make any commitments.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements or accessibility needs?

Absolutely! We pride ourselves on creating inclusive travel experiences. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions, mobility concerns, or other special requirements, and we'll ensure they're accommodated throughout your journey.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule my trip?

We understand that plans can change. We provide clear cancellation and rescheduling policies for each booking, and we always recommend travel insurance to protect your investment. Our team will work with you to minimize any penalties or fees whenever possible.
